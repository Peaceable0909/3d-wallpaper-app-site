Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 3D Wallpaper Studio ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with this website and our associated Pinterest presence.

This website does not require account creation and does not collect personal information beyond what you voluntarily provide, such as when you message us directly on Pinterest. If we add an email newsletter or download form in the future, we will collect only the information needed for that feature and will update this policy before doing so.

Use of the Pinterest API

We use the Pinterest API to publish our own wallpaper content to our own Pinterest account. We do not use the Pinterest API to access, collect, or store data belonging to Pinterest users, and we do not request any permissions beyond what is required to publish content to our own account.

Cookies and analytics

This site may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand overall traffic (for example, page views). We do not use this data to identify individual visitors, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

Third-party links

This site links to third-party platforms, including Pinterest. Those platforms have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review separately.

Data retention

We retain any information you send us directly only as long as needed to respond to you or as required by law.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact us
